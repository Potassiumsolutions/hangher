How to Play

The Game

Guess the hidden word one letter at a time. Each wrong guess adds a piece to the figure. Reveal the full word before all 9 pieces appear — Head, Pigtails (×2), Body, Arms (×2), Skirt, Legs (×2) — or she's hanged.

Category

Choose the theme for your word:
Animals — creatures great and small
Countries — nations of the world
Food & Drink — from ingredients to dishes
Science — elements, concepts, and vocabulary
Around the House — furniture, tools, and everyday items
🎲 Random (online) — fetches a real random word from the internet. Requires a connection.

Mode

Easy — picks any word from the selected category.
Hard — avoids common letters. The letters R, S, T, L, N, and E are the most frequent in English — in Hard mode, words containing the fewest of these are chosen, so your usual go-to guesses won't help much.

Word Length

Filter words to a specific letter count — from 3 up to 12 — or leave it on Any to let the game choose freely. Shorter words are trickier; longer words give you more letters to work with.

Controls

New Word — start a fresh round at any time.
Give Up — reveal the answer and end the current round.
On desktop you can also press any letter key to guess, and Enter to start a new word.
